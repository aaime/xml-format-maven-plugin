This plugin available from Maven Central. This means you can simply add it to your pom.xml, specifying your preferred Maven lifecycle phase and formatting configuration. For example:

<plugin>
  <groupId>au.com.acegi.xmlformat</groupId>
  <artifactId>xml-format-maven-plugin</artifactId>
  <version>3.0.0-SNAPSHOT</version>
  <executions>
    <execution>
      <id>xml-format</id>
      <phase>package</phase>
        <goals>
          <goal>xml-format</goal>
        </goals>
      <configuration>
        <!-- configure your formatting preferences here (see link below) -->
        <indentSize>2</indentSize>
      </configuration>
    </execution>
  </executions>
</plugin>

The above will cause mvn package to format all your XML files automatically. Please see the xml-format:xml-format page for the available configuration options.
